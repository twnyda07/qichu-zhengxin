佛問阿難：「唯心與目，今何所在？」阿難七度指出心的所在，世尊七度破之，最後皆「無有是處」——心了不可得。一頁看懂這場論辯的全貌。 The Buddha asked Ānanda where his mind is. Seven times Ānanda names a location; seven times the Buddha shows it is impossible. The whole debate, at a glance.

🔍 破的是哪種心？Which mind?

七處所指的，都是會生滅的「妄心」；要顯的是不生滅的「真心」。
All seven point to the perishable false mind; the goal is the everlasting true mind.
妄心 · 緣塵分別
False mind
  • 隨外境生、隨外境滅
  • Arises & ceases with objects
  • 七處遍尋，了不可得
  • Sought in 7 places—found nowhere
  • 阿難誤認的「我心」
  • What Ānanda mistook for “my mind”
真心 · 常住性淨
True mind
  • 不生不滅、常住
  • Neither arises nor ceases
  • 性淨明體、本自具足
  • Pure, bright, complete in itself
  • 破妄之後方能顯現
  • Revealed once the false is dissolved
宣化上人：阿難把生滅的妄心當作真心，正是 「認賊為子」。 Master Hsüan Hua: mistaking the false mind for the true is “taking a burglar for one’s own child.”
