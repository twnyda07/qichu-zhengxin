七 處 徵 心 Where Is the Mind? · The Seven Searches

佛問阿難：「唯心與目，今何所在？」
阿難七度指出心的所在，佛七度破之——
看你能不能跟上世尊的推理，認出真心。 The Buddha asked Ānanda, “Where exactly are your mind and your eyes?”
Seven times Ānanda names a location for the mind; seven times the Buddha refutes it.
Can you follow the World-Honored One’s reasoning and recognize the true mind?

☸️
世尊 破斥
The Buddha refutes
七 處 皆 非　·　心 不 可 得 The Mind Is Found in None of the Seven
阿難七次所指，都是「緣塵分別」的妄心——隨外境生、隨外境滅的識心。佛七番徵問，不是要找出妄心藏在哪裡，而是要當下拆穿它「了不可得」，好讓常住真心、性淨明體顯現。
宣化上人云：阿難仍把生滅的妄心當作真心，正是「認賊為子」。 All seven of Ānanda’s answers point to the conditioned, discriminating mind—the consciousness that arises and ceases along with perceived objects. The Buddha’s sevenfold questioning is not meant to locate that false mind, but to show on the spot that it cannot be found at all, so that the everlasting true mind, the pure bright essence of one’s nature, may come to light. As Master Hsüan Hua says, Ānanda still mistakes his deluded mind for his true mind—he has “mistaken a burglar for his own child.”
課堂省思
For reflection
  1. 阿難七個答案，是否都暗藏同一個錯誤前提——把「心」當成一個有「位置」的東西？Do all seven answers hide the same false assumption—that the mind is a thing with a “location”?
  2. 佛陀「七番破處」，破的是哪一種心？要顯的又是哪一種心？Which mind is the Buddha dismantling, and which mind is he pointing to?
  3. 此刻，你「以為」你的心在哪裡？這個「以為」本身，是真心還是妄心？Right now, where do you *suppose* your mind is? Is that very supposing the true mind, or the false one?
